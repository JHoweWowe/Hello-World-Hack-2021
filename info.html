So What is Hello World Hack?

This year's Hello World Hack is a 48 hour, beginner-orientated 100% online hackathon. If you've never attended a hackathon, or recently picked up programming and want to get a project off the ground, this is the hackathon for you! Filled with workshops on setting up a project, building it, and testing it, awesome prizes and high-quality banter galore! The best part, it's all free!
