So What exactly is Hello World Hack?

This year's Hello World Hack is a 48 hour, beginner-orientated 100% online hackathon.
If you've never attended a hackathon, or recently picked up programming and want to get a project off the ground, this is the hackathon for you! Filled with workshops on setting up a project, building it, and testing it, awesome prizes and high-quality banter galore! The best part, it's all free!

So what are you waiting for?

Not a Beginner? Become a Mentor!

Mentors are a vital part of Hello World Hack. Each team will be assigned a mentor to help them with their project. As a mentor you will work closely with the teams while also getting an opportunity to chat to sponsors. If you have been to a hackathon before or have work experience then we encourage you to get involved!
